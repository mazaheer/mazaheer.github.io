Learned that a holomorphic function on an open set has a power series expansion and that the radius of the disc of convergence can be made large as long as the closure lies in the domain. This follows simply and elegantly from the Cauchy integral formula. The higher Cauchy integral formulae also follow from this by considering the coefficients. As a consequence we get the Fundamental Theorem of Algebra.
