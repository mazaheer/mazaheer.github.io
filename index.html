Muhammad Atif Zaheer

I am a second-year graduate student in mathematics at Pennsylvania State University.

I am primarily interested in analytic number theory, particularly in the pair correlation of the zeros of the Riemann zeta function and the higher derivative of the Riemann Xi-function, moments of the Riemann zeta function, zero-free regions and zero-density estimates of L-functions, and the distribution of primes. My interests also extend to algebraic number theory and algebraic geometry.
