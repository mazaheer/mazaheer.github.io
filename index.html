About Me

I am a second-year graduate student in mathematics at Penn State where I am working with Amita Malik. I completed M.S. in Mathematics from Lahore University of Management Sciences, Lahore, Pakistan in 2024 under the supervision of Emanuel Carneiro. Before that I did B.S. in Mathematics from National University of of Sciences and Technology, Islamabad, Pakistan.

I am interested in analytic number theory, particularly in pair correlation of zeros of Riemann zeta function and derivatives of completed Dirichlet L-functions.
