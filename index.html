I am associate professor at SISSA, Trieste, in the group Geometry and Mathematical Physics.

I work in Algebraic Geometry, mostly on enumerative geometry of moduli spaces of sheaves and their invariants. I am also interested in moduli spaces of curves, derived categories of coherent sheaves, semiorthogonal decompositions and Fourier–Mukai transforms.
