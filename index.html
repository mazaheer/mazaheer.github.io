Muhammad Atif Zaheer

I am a second-year graduate student in mathematics at Pennsylvania State University working under Amita Malik. Prior to this I obtained my M.S. in Mathematics from Lahore University of Management Sciences, Lahore, Pakistan in 2024. I did my master's thesis under Emanuel Carneiro and it was titled On Pair Correlation Conjecture and Bounds for the Averages of Montgomery's F-function. I completed B.S. in Mathematics from National University of Sciences and Technology, Islamabad, Pakistan in 2022.

I am primarily interested in analytic number theory, particularly in the pair correlation of the zeros of the Riemann zeta function and the derivatives of the completed Riemann zeta function, moments of the Riemann zeta function, zero-free regions and zero-density estimates of L-functions, and distribution of primes.

Arithmetic Functions

Fun problem. Two circles A and B are externally tangent. The radius of circle A is one-third that of circle B. Circle A rolls without slipping around circle B. How many complete rotations does circle A make before returning to its starting position?
