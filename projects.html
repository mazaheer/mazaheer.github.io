1. Zeros of Derivatives of L-functions

I study quantitative estimates for the number and distribution of zeros of derivatives of Dirichlet L-functions and related ξ-functions. This includes bounding expressions of the form \( \frac{\xi''}{\xi'}(s,\chi) \) in vertical strips.

Techniques involve contour integration, zero-density estimates, and bounds for logarithmic derivatives.

2. Montgomery’s Pair Correlation and F-functions

This project investigates average bounds for Montgomery's F-functions and their relation to the pair correlation conjecture.

The goal is to understand implications for fine-scale statistics of zeros of the Riemann zeta function.

3. Analytic Properties of the Riemann Zeta Function

I work on explicit bounds for \( \zeta(s) \), \( \zeta'(s) \), and related Dirichlet series in vertical strips.

This includes applications of the Phragmén–Lindelöf principle and complex analytic methods in analytic number theory.
